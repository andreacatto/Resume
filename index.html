  • Physichs Engineer-Politecnico di Milano/ IIT

    • 2022-2023

    As part of a research group in collaboration with IIT and Polimi, our team undertook the responsibility for the fabrication and characterization of a photonic/plasmonic crystal Proof of Concept (PoC) for bacterial population sensing. This endeavor aimed to demonstrate the technological validity of the device, showcasing its potential utility in both food and biomedical applications. Below, the specific contributions made during this project:

    • Substrate Preparation: Preparation of the substrate utilizing plasma cleaning techniques. This step ensured the removal of any contaminants, providing a clean surface for subsequent processes.
    • Dispersion Preparation and Stabilization:Handled the preparation and stabilization of silica and titania dispersions. To prevent the formation of nanoparticle clusters use of a two step process involving sonication and tip sonication.
    • DBR/Crystal Creation via Spin Coating: Fabrication of the Distributed Bragg Reflector (DBR) and crystal structure, employing the spin coating technique.
    • PVD Deposition for Nanostructured Ag Layer: Deposition a nanostructured silver (Ag) layer to create the photonic crystal, utilizing Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). This process enabled precise control over the deposition parameters, resulting in the desired nanostructure for plasmonic effects.
    • UV-Vis Spectroscopy for Plasmon Resonance Frequency: UV-Vis spectroscopy to determine the resonance frequency of the Tamm plasmon. This analysis provided crucial insights into the device s optical characteristics before and after exposure to bacterial populations.
    • EM Analysis for Surface Morphology Observation: Examination of the surface morphology of the device pre and post exposure to bacterial populations. Using Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM). This enabled detailed observation of any structural changes induced by bacterial interactions.
    • Data Analysis and Team Coordination: Data analysis to extract meaningful insights from experimental results. Closely collaboration with team members to identify areas for improvement and implement necessary modifications to enhance the device s performance and functionality.
